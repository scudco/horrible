horrible by scudco

Horrible helps you integrate manual and automated specs/tests

Description

Horrible helps you integrate specifications(or tests) which need to be verified manually into your automated specs/tests.

Features

  • Manual specs with descriptions and steps
  • Steps are presented as a prompt
  • Steps can contain blocks of code to be executed in context of the spec
 1 require 'rubygems'
 2 require 'horrible'
 3 
 4 describe "A horrible spec" do
 5 
 6   before :all do
 7     @hokeypokey = false
 8   end
 9 
10   it "should be true" do
11     true.should be_true
12   end
13 
14   you "should verify that the sky is blue" do
15     step("What color is the sky?").should == "blue"
16     step "Now do the hokey-pokey" do
17       @hokeypokey = true
18     end
19     @hokeypokey.should be_true
20   end
21 
22 end
